Fraud Detection

Learn about the different types of fraud detection methods and how they can help businesses identify fraudulent activities effectively.

Types of Fraud Detection

Credit Card Fraud

Credit card fraud occurs when someone uses a stolen or fake credit card to make unauthorized purchases. Detecting this type of fraud involves monitoring transaction patterns for suspicious activity, such as unusual locations or high transaction amounts.

Identity Theft

Identity theft involves using someone else's personal information (like Social Security numbers or bank account details) to commit fraud. This is often detected by analyzing changes in personal data usage or monitoring for fraudulent activity.

Insurance Fraud

Insurance fraud occurs when a person lies or misrepresents information to receive an undue benefit from an insurance policy. This can include fake claims or exaggerating the damage to property. Detecting such fraud involves comparing claims data to historical trends and analyzing any anomalies.
